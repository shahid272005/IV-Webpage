About the Visit

Linking Classroom Concepts with Real-World Systems

This industrial visit is designed to give CSE & AIML students a clear picture of how software development, embedded systems, and large-scale IT services are executed in professional environments.

Objectives

The visit focuses on bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry practices, helping students understand:

  • How modern software and embedded products are designed and tested.
  • Real-time workflows followed in large IT organizations.
  • Industry expectations from entry-level engineers.
  • Importance of soft skills, teamwork and documentation.
